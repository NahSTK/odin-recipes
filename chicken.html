Baked chicken legs

We have covered how to make some tasty baked potatoes and now we will see how to couple that with it's undefeated perfect match wich is as tasty as it is nutricious.

Ingredients and Cookware

For this recipe you will need:

  • 2 small chicken legs (per person)
  • 1 baking dish
  • Some sweet smoked paprika
  • Some romera
  • Some parsley

Recipe

Now onto the cooking:

  1. Prepare the plate. Put a little touch of olive oil like iff you were guided by Gordom Ramsay. This is adviced to avoid the skin sticking to the plate while in the oven.
  2. Under the skin. Embrace the skin on your chicken by using it to your advantage. Roll it back a bit, season with paprika, romera and a touch of parsley, allowing flavour to sink deep into the fibers.
  3. Don't forget the outside. Seasoning the outside of your chicken leg is also a good idea to have a crispy and tasty sking to bite into later.
  4. Baking. With a pre-heted oven at low heat, throw the chicken legs inside. Remember you can combine this with this other recipe to make some company for your plate in the oven.
  5. ENJOY! Since this meal has a similar baking time you can expect 1 and a half hours before it's ready too. So you can have a good timed side dish to eat with your crispy chicken.
